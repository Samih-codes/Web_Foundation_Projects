The Best Anime Series According to Samih

My top 3 anime of all-time

Naruto

The first anime I ever watched! An epic tale of ninjas, friendship, and growth resonates deeply
with its character development and intricate plot twists.

Attack on Titan

Unforgettable for its intense storyline and gripping suspense, drawing me in with its profound
exploration of humanity and survival.

Kurokos Basketball

A captivating storyline, memorable characters, and adrenaline-pumping basketball matches,
resonating deeply with my love for sports.
